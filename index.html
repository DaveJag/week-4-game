CrystalsCollector!

At the beginning of each game, you are given a target number, between 19-120. Your objective is to make your score exactly match this number.

  • Each crystal carries a value between 1-12.
  • Clicking on a crystal adds this amount to your score.
  • Crystal values change for each game.
  • More than 1 crystal may have the same value.
